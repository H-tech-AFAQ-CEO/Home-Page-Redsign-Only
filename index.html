MEET OUR TEAM

Meet Your Chilliwack
Dental Team

Dr. Don Utas

Our team at Sardis Riverside Dental clinic is dedicated to providing you with the highest quality dental care possible. We are a highly-educated dental professional team with a diverse set of skills that ensure you receive the personalized care you and your family truly deserve.

Dr. Don Utas received his Dental degree from the University of Saskatchewan in 1994 and bought Meadowbrook Dental in Chilliwack a year later. As a lifelong learner, he has earned a Diploma of Technology at BCIT and a Bachelor of Education at Simon Fraser University.

Since 2006, Dr. Utas has been committed to offering the most up-to-date technology to his patients through laser dentistry. He received Mastership Certification in Laser Dentistry at the World Clinical Laser Institute in California.
